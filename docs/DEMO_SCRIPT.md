# TRINETRA Demo Video Script (3–4 minutes)

**Suggested title on screen:** TRINETRA — AI Digital Public Safety Intelligence  
**Hackathon:** CRP ET AI Hackathon 2026 · Problem Statement 6  
**Length target:** 3:30

---

## 0:00–0:25 · Hook + problem

**Visual:** Landing page hero, scroll stats.

**Narration:**  
“In 2024, digital arrest scams alone stole over seventeen hundred crore rupees from Indian families — often over multi-day video calls where fraudsters pose as CBI or ED. Banks still struggle with high-quality fake notes. Police get the FIR *after* the money has vanished.

TRINETRA flips that doctrine: intelligence at the *point of contact*, not the point of complaint.”

---

## 0:25–0:55 · Solution overview

**Visual:** Click through module cards on landing; open Command Centre.

**Narration:**  
“TRINETRA is a three-eye intelligence fabric — citizens, banks, and law enforcement — fused into one platform. Six live modules: Command Centre, Scam Detector, Currency Agent, Fraud Network Graph, Geospatial Intel, and Citizen Shield.”

**Show KPIs:** Active threats, ₹18.4 Cr prevented, 6.4 hour lead time, 1.8% false positive rate.

---

## 0:55–1:40 · Scam Detector (hero demo)

**Visual:** Scam Detector → sample “Digital arrest” → show critical score, signals, MHA alert draft. Then switch to “Safe bank SMS” for contrast.

**Narration:**  
“Here is a real digital-arrest script pattern. TRINETRA’s NLP agent scores it critical — agency impersonation, arrest coercion, isolation instructions, urgent transfer. Every signal is explainable. With one click we draft an MHA-style cyber alert for telecom and bank FRA — *before* the UPI happens.

A legitimate bank SMS scores safe. That low false-positive rate matters when citizens trust the tool.”

---

## 1:40–2:15 · Currency Agent

**Visual:** Load Counterfeit ₹500 → red COUNTERFEIT verdict → feature fails. Switch Genuine ₹500 → green.

**Narration:**  
“For field officers and bank tellers, the Currency Agent scores microprint, security thread, watermark, UV response, print noise and edge sharpness. Counterfeit notes fail multiple features; genuine notes pass consensus. Same schema can sit on a mobile camera or POS device.”

---

## 2:15–2:50 · Network + Geospatial

**Visual:** Fraud Network — click Compound Alpha, follow mule path. Geospatial — select Bengaluru / Indore.

**Narration:**  
“Graph intelligence links the compound to handlers, spoofed numbers, money mules and victims across states — packaged for court-admissible handoff.

Geospatial heatmaps rank hotspots so command centres prioritise cyber patrols and share intel across districts in near real time.”

---

## 2:50–3:20 · Citizen Shield

**Visual:** Citizen chat — paste digital arrest text → verdict + 1930 guidance.

**Narration:**  
“For every citizen — WhatsApp, IVR, or app — Citizen Shield walks you through risk assessment in plain language and guides reporting on 1930 and cybercrime.gov.in. Multilingual advisory is on the roadmap.”

---

## 3:20–3:45 · Close / impact

**Visual:** Back to Command Centre or landing tagline.

**Narration:**  
“TRINETRA turns fragmented complaints into proactive, multi-agency intelligence. Less hostage time. Fewer successful transfers. Faster disruption of industrialised scam compounds.

Three eyes. One truth. Thank you.”

---

## Recording tips

- Browser fullscreen 1920×1080, dark UI as-is  
- Cursor highlighter on; avoid rushing clicks  
- Optional soft background score at −25 dB  
- Export MP4 H.264, under platform size limits  
- Upload to YouTube/Drive unlisted; paste link in Unstop submission
